Communication

Communication & information supply is an essence of any long-lasting relationship so it is in your relationship with your neighbours within the society. JustMyNeighbour gives your community various alternatives to discuss, chat, complain, take opinions, interact and distribute information. The highlights here are: Online Notice Boards - for members to view new updates, Discussion Forum - a platform for residents to discuss common issues and encourage resident participation, Opinion Polls - for taking quick decisions, Complaints - a section to put forth your concern & check its status, Weekly digest, Events, and many more.

Facility Managaement

Organization and facilitation are the two important aspects of societies. 1CN helps them to do exactly that! Complete automation in operations brings in clear accountability. It helps in optimizing society resources and you can easily track the inventory status. This standard system brings in discipline and structure to an establishment. Every resident is aware of the processes of the society management and can thus contribute.
